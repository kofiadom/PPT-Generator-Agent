### Slide 3: Problem Breakdown
**Template Index:** 2 (Three-Column Problem Statement)
**Content Section:** Understanding the Problem (Items 1-3)
**Key Points:**
- **Item 1:** Fragmented Providers & Processes (12 providers, 7 file templates, missed tax filings)

- **Item 3:** Limited Visibility & Analytics (10-day lag on KPIs, slow decision-making)

### Slide 4: Project Objective
**Template Index:** 3 (Objective Statement)
**Content Section:** Understanding the Problem - Project Objective
**Key Points:**
- SMART objective: By June 30, 2026, standardize and automate Acme's payroll across 12 countries
- Target: 99.8% accuracy, ≤2-day close, ≥75% ticket reduction, 18% cost decrease
- Sustained for three consecutive cycles
