**High Flexibility:**
- Slides 1, 3, 4: Easily adaptable to various text lengths
- Slide 2: Can accommodate 2-4 categories by adjusting column widths

**Moderate Flexibility:**
- Slides 5, 6: Work best with balanced content on both sides
- Slide 8: Optimal for 4 items, but can be adapted for 2-3

**Structured Formats:**
- Slide 7: Requires data preparation; chart area needs specific formatting
- Slide 9: Best for 6-12 month timelines; may need adjustment for longer/shorter periods
- Slide 10: Designed for 4 team members; scaling requires layout modification

**Content Volume Guidance:**
- Slides 2, 6, 7: Support detailed content with multiple bullet points
- Slides 3, 4: Best for concise, impactful statements
- Slide 8: Works best with 2-3 bullets per deliverable
- Slide 10: Accommodate 2-4 sentence bios per person

**Visual Elements:**
- Slide 7: Only slide with embedded data visualization
- Slide 10: Only slide with image placeholders (headshots)
- All other slides: Text-focused with minimal graphics
