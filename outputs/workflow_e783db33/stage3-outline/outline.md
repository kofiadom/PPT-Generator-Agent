### Slide 7 (Final Deck) → Template Slide 6: Market Trends
**Content:** Two key trends with implications
- **Trend 1:** Consolidation from multi-vendor to global platforms
  - 40%+ of mid-market globals plan consolidation by 2027
  - Implications: Leverage scale, standard controls, contract simplicity
- **Trend 2:** Real-time pay and self-service analytics
  - Accelerating adoption for near-real-time accruals
  - Implications: Deploy dashboards and pre-pay variance alerts

- Chart data: Payroll errors declining from 7.5 (2022) to 2.0 target (2026)
